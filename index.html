About Me

A marina (from Spanish [maˈɾina], Portuguese [maˈɾinɐ] and Italian [maˈriːna]: marina, "coast" or "shore") is a dock or basin with moorings and supplies for yachts and small boats. Marinas may be located along the banks of rivers connecting to lakes or seas and may be inland.

The name Marina is of Latin origin, and is used in more than one countries and different languages of the world, especially Bulgarian speaking countries, English speaking countries, German speaking countries, Greek speaking countries among others.
